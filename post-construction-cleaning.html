What We Do

Here's Exactly What's Included

Post-construction cleaning is a multi-phase process. We go room by room, surface by surface, until every inch of the space is clean and ready.

Debris & Trash Removal

  • All construction debris removed
  • Cardboard, packaging, and offcuts cleared
  • Drywall dust and scraps removed
  • Space cleared and staged for deep clean

Dust Removal — Walls to Floors

  • All surfaces dusted from ceiling down
  • Vents, sills, and ledges cleaned
  • Drywall and concrete dust fully removed
  • HEPA vacuuming used throughout

Window & Glass Cleaning

  • Construction film and stickers removed
  • Paint overspray and adhesive removed
  • Interior and exterior glass cleaned
  • Frames, tracks, and sills wiped clean

Floor Cleaning & Prep

  • Protective covering removed and floors cleaned
  • Concrete dust scrubbed and removed
  • Adhesive and grout residue cleaned
  • Hard floors scrubbed, vinyl and tile polished

Restrooms & Kitchen Areas

  • All fixtures cleaned and disinfected
  • Construction residue removed from sinks and toilets
  • Cabinetry wiped inside and out
  • Countertops cleaned and ready for use

Final Detail & Touch-Up Clean

  • Room-by-room final inspection
  • Light switches, outlets, and hardware wiped
  • Any remaining marks or residue addressed
  • Space handed over inspection-ready
