Common Questions

Carpet Cleaning FAQ

Common questions from office managers and business owners about commercial carpet cleaning.

Most commercial carpets benefit from a professional deep clean every 3–6 months. High-traffic areas like lobbies, hallways, and break rooms may need cleaning more often. Medical offices and places with strict hygiene requirements often do monthly deep cleans. We'll assess your carpet and recommend the right schedule.

Typically 2–4 hours with our professional equipment and air movers. Arizona's dry climate actually helps — carpets dry faster here than in more humid states. When we clean in the evening, carpets are almost always dry and ready for the next business day.

Many stains can be significantly reduced or removed with proper treatment. The results depend on the type of stain, how long it's been there, and the carpet fiber type. We pre-treat every stain and use the right products for each type. We'll always be honest with you about what's realistic before we start.

Hot water extraction (sometimes called steam cleaning) is the most thorough method — it flushes dirt, bacteria, and allergens out of the carpet fibers. Dry cleaning uses less moisture and dries faster, but doesn't clean as deeply. For commercial properties that need a true deep clean, we recommend hot water extraction.

Yes. We use commercial-grade, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for people, pets, and the environment once dry. If your facility has specific sensitivities or requires fragrance-free or chemical-free products, just let us know and we'll accommodate.

Absolutely. You don't have to do the whole building at once. Many clients have us deep clean high-traffic areas more frequently and do a full building clean less often. We can work in sections, by floor, or by room — whatever makes the most sense for your property and budget.
