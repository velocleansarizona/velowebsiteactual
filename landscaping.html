Why Choose Velo

Why Commercial Properties in Maricopa County Choose Us

Landscaping in Arizona isn't the same as landscaping anywhere else. The heat, the monsoons, the hard water, and the desert plants all require specific knowledge. We've been working in this climate long enough to know what works and what doesn't.

01

We Know Arizona's Climate.

We understand the difference between a summer watering schedule and a winter one. We know which plants thrive in Maricopa County heat and which ones need extra attention during monsoon season. You get local expertise, not generic advice.

02

We Keep Your Water Bill in Check.

Overwatering is one of the most expensive landscaping mistakes in Arizona. We calibrate irrigation systems to the right zones, right times, and right amounts — keeping your plants healthy without wasting water.

03

Consistent, Reliable Service.

We show up on the same day every week or every two weeks — no guessing when we'll come. Your grounds will always be clean and maintained before your clients or tenants arrive.

04

One Company for Everything Outside.

We also do pressure washing and line striping — so your entire exterior can be managed by a single vendor. No juggling multiple companies or schedules.

Landscaping FAQ

Questions about keeping your commercial grounds looking great in Arizona? We have answers.

Most commercial properties with turf grass need weekly service during the warmer months and bi-weekly in winter. Desert landscaping properties can typically be maintained bi-weekly or monthly depending on weed pressure and debris. We'll visit your property and recommend the right schedule to keep it looking sharp year-round.

Both. In fact, most commercial properties in Maricopa County have a mix of desert rock, decomposed granite, desert plants, and some turf areas. We handle all of it — raking and refreshing rock, maintaining desert plants and cacti, trimming shrubs, and mowing any grass areas. We know Arizona landscapes inside and out.

Yes. We inspect and maintain drip systems, sprinkler heads, and irrigation controllers as part of our landscape maintenance. We'll check for broken heads, clogged drip emitters, leaks, and make sure your system is running efficiently. Proper irrigation in Arizona makes a huge difference in plant health and your water bill.

Yes. Monsoon season can leave a mess — branches down, desert debris everywhere, flooded mulch areas, and damaged plants. We offer post-monsoon cleanup as a one-time service or as part of your regular maintenance plan. Just give us a call after a big storm and we'll get your property back in shape.

Yes. We apply pre-emergent weed control treatments at the right times of year to prevent weeds before they come up — which is much more effective than pulling them after the fact. We also do targeted post-emergent treatments when needed, using products that are safe for your landscaping and the surrounding environment.
