What We Do

Here's Exactly What's Included

Every area of your healthcare facility gets the attention it needs — with the right products and the right procedures.

Exam & Treatment Rooms

  • Tables and equipment surfaces disinfected
  • Paper rolls replaced as needed
  • High-touch areas thoroughly sanitized
  • Floors mopped with hospital-grade solution

Waiting Rooms & Reception

  • Chairs, armrests, and tables wiped and sanitized
  • Reception desk and counter cleaned
  • Entry doors and high-touch surfaces disinfected
  • Floors vacuumed or mopped

Restrooms

  • Toilets, sinks, and mirrors disinfected
  • Floors sanitized with medical-grade cleaner
  • Soap, paper towels, and supplies restocked
  • Trash emptied and liners replaced

High-Touch Surface Disinfection

  • Door handles, light switches, and outlets
  • Keyboards, phones, and shared equipment
  • Chair arms, handrails, and countertops
  • Sign-in pads, clipboards, and pens

Biohazard-Aware Procedures

  • Team trained in bloodborne pathogen awareness
  • PPE worn during all healthcare cleans
  • Proper disposal of contaminated materials
  • Full compliance with OSHA standards

Products & Standards

  • EPA-registered, hospital-grade disinfectants
  • CDC and OSHA cleaning guidelines followed
  • Microfiber cloths to prevent cross-contamination
  • Color-coded supplies per room type
