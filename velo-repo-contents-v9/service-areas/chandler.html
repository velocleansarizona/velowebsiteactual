Common Questions

Chandler Commercial Cleaning FAQ

How quickly can Velo start service at my Chandler property?

For most Chandler commercial properties, we can schedule a walk-through within a few business days of your inquiry and start recurring service within one to two weeks. For urgent one-time jobs — pressure washing before a grand opening, post-construction cleanup, or a last-minute event turnover — we can often fit you in within days.

Do you offer after-hours or overnight cleaning in Chandler?

Yes. The majority of our Chandler office, medical, and retail clients are cleaned outside business hours — nights, early mornings, or weekends. We build our schedules around when your property is closed so your team, patients, and customers are never disrupted.

What types of Chandler businesses do you serve?

Tech and semiconductor campuses along the price corridor, corporate office buildings, medical and dental offices in ocotillo and downtown, retail centers around chandler fashion center, warehouses, and mixed-use properties throughout the city.

Are you licensed and insured to work in Chandler commercial buildings?

Yes. Velo Property Solutions is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We can provide certificates of insurance for property managers, landlords, and building owners on request — including additional insured endorsements when required.
