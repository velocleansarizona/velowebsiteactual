Why Choose Velo

Why Businesses in Maricopa County Choose Us for Window Cleaning

Arizona is hard on windows. Dust storms coat them in a layer of grit. Monsoon rains leave mineral deposits. Hard water from sprinklers leaves white spots that don't come off on their own. We deal with all of it — and we know exactly how to handle Arizona's specific conditions.

01

Streak-Free, Every Single Time.

We use professional-grade squeegees, pure water systems, and the right techniques to leave your glass completely streak-free. No cloudy finish, no water spots — just clear glass.

02

We Handle Arizona's Hard Water.

Maricopa County has some of the hardest water in the country. Sprinkler overspray and mineral buildup can permanently etch glass if left untreated. We use specialized solutions to remove deposits and restore your windows before the damage sets in.

03

We Do Inside & Outside in One Visit.

We clean both sides in a single appointment so you're not scheduling two separate visits or dealing with half-clean windows. One visit, complete results.

04

Safe at Any Height.

We use water-fed pole systems to safely clean upper-story windows from the ground — no unsafe ladders leaning against your building. For taller structures, we use the right equipment for the job.
