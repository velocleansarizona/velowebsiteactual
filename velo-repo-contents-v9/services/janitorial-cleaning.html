Here's Exactly What's Included

No surprises. No hidden extras. This is what our janitorial team takes care of at every visit.

Restroom Cleaning

  • Toilets, sinks, and mirrors cleaned
  • Floors mopped and sanitized
  • Soap, paper towels, and supplies restocked
  • Trash emptied and liners replaced

Common Areas & Lobbies

  • Floors vacuumed or mopped
  • Surfaces dusted and wiped down
  • Entry doors and glass cleaned
  • Trash emptied and areas tidied

Break Rooms & Kitchens

  • Counters, tables, and appliances wiped
  • Sink cleaned and sanitized
  • Microwave and fridge exterior cleaned
  • Floors swept and mopped

Floors & Carpets

  • Carpets vacuumed thoroughly
  • Entry mats cleaned
  • Baseboards dusted

Trash & Recycling

  • Liners replaced every visit
  • Recycling separated properly
