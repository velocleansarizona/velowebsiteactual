Why Choose Velo

Why Property Owners in Maricopa County Choose Us

Arizona is tough on properties. Dust storms, monsoon season, blazing heat, and heavy traffic leave surfaces looking worn. We know what it takes to clean in this climate — and we bring the right equipment to get real results.

01

We Use the Right Pressure for Every Surface.

Too much pressure can damage concrete, stucco, or paint. Too little and it won't clean. We assess each surface and use the right PSI and technique — no damage, just clean.

02

Hot Water for the Toughest Stains.

Grease, oil, and built-up grime don't always come off with cold water alone. We offer hot water pressure washing for loading docks, dumpster pads, and drive-throughs where it really matters.

03

We Protect Your Surfaces Long-Term.

Regular pressure washing removes buildup that breaks down concrete and asphalt over time. Keeping surfaces clean actually extends their life and saves you money on repairs down the road.

04

We Schedule Around Your Business.

Most clients prefer early morning or evening cleaning so the work is done before customers arrive. We work around your hours — the property is clean and dry before your doors open.

Pressure Washing FAQ

Everything you want to know about commercial pressure washing for your Maricopa County property.

For most commercial properties in Arizona, we recommend pressure washing at least twice a year — once before summer monsoon season and once after. High-traffic areas like drive-throughs, loading docks, and dumpster pads may need monthly or quarterly service. We'll help you figure out the right schedule for your property.

Not when done correctly. We assess every surface before we start and adjust our pressure settings and nozzle type accordingly. Concrete can handle higher pressure, while surfaces like painted walls, stucco, or older brick need a softer approach. We know the difference — and we're careful every time.

Pressure washing uses high-powered water to blast away dirt and stains — great for concrete, asphalt, and tough surfaces. Soft washing uses lower pressure combined with cleaning solutions — better for building exteriors, stucco, painted surfaces, and roofs where high pressure could cause damage. We offer both, and we'll use the right method for your surface.
