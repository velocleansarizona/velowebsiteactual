Every corner of your office gets cleaned — from the front desk to the back hallway. Here's what we take care of at every visit.

Workstations & Desks

  • Desks and workstations wiped and dusted
  • Keyboards, monitors, and phones sanitized

Restrooms

  • Baseboards and edges dusted
