Our Story

Built to Serve Maricopa County Businesses

Velo Property Solutions was founded with one goal: give commercial properties in Maricopa County a cleaning and maintenance company they can actually count on. A team that shows up, does the work right, and communicates clearly — every visit.

We've grown by doing exactly that. Word of mouth from happy clients is what drives our business. From restaurants and offices to medical facilities and warehouses, we've built a reputation for being the team that businesses trust to keep their properties in top shape.

We're not a national franchise or a faceless corporation. We're a local team that genuinely cares about the businesses we serve. When you call Velo, you talk to real people who know your property and take pride in the work they do.
