Best Commercial Cleaning Companies in Phoenix, AZ (2025 Guide)

June 2025 8 min read Phoenix, AZ Velo Property Solutions

If you're searching for a commercial cleaning company in Phoenix, you already know there are a lot of options. The challenge isn't finding a cleaning company — it's finding one that actually shows up consistently, does quality work, and communicates clearly when something needs to change.

This guide covers what to look for in a commercial cleaning company in Phoenix and Maricopa County, what questions to ask before you sign anything, and the most important things that separate the best companies from the rest.

Quick answer: The best commercial cleaning companies in Phoenix are locally owned, fully licensed and insured, have verified Google reviews, offer custom service plans, and have experience with your specific type of facility.

What to Look for in a Phoenix Commercial Cleaning Company

Before we get into specifics, here are the non-negotiables when evaluating any commercial cleaning company in the Phoenix area:

  • Licensed, bonded, and insured — This is the bare minimum. If something gets damaged or an employee gets injured on your property, you need to know you're protected. Never hire an unlicensed cleaner for your business.
  • Background-checked employees — Your cleaning team will often have access to your building outside of business hours. Background checks are essential.
  • Verified reviews on Google — Look for consistent 4–5 star reviews over a period of time. A handful of old reviews isn't enough. Recent, detailed reviews are a better signal.
  • Experience with your type of facility — Cleaning a medical office is very different from cleaning a warehouse. Make sure the company has specific experience with your property type.
  • Transparent pricing — A reputable company will give you a clear, written quote. Be cautious of companies that can't give you pricing without a lot of back-and-forth.

Local vs. National Commercial Cleaning Companies in Phoenix

One of the biggest decisions Phoenix businesses face is whether to hire a local cleaning company or go with a national franchise. Here's how they compare:

National Franchise Cleaning Companies

National franchise cleaners are large organizations that sell territories to local operators. The brand name can feel reassuring, but the actual service quality varies enormously by franchise owner. Some of the downsides Phoenix businesses report:

  • High staff turnover means a different team showing up each time
  • Customer service goes through a call center, not a local contact
  • Cookie-cutter service plans that don't fit your specific needs
  • Slower response times when you need to make changes
  • Higher prices due to franchise fees being passed to clients

Locally Owned Commercial Cleaning Companies in Phoenix

Local cleaning companies like Velo Property Solutions are run by people who live and work in Maricopa County. The advantages are significant:

  • Direct line to the owner or manager — real accountability
  • Consistent crews who get to know your property
  • Custom plans built for your specific facility
  • Faster response when you need schedule changes or extra services
  • Local reputation matters — they have more to lose by doing bad work

Questions to Ask Before Hiring a Commercial Cleaning Company

Before you commit to any commercial cleaning company in Phoenix, ask these questions:

  • Are you licensed, bonded, and insured? — Ask for proof. A legitimate company will provide it without hesitation.
  • Do your employees pass background checks? — Essential if they'll have keys or after-hours access.
  • Will we have the same crew every visit? — Consistency matters. Rotating staff means lower quality and less familiarity with your space.
  • What cleaning products do you use? — Especially important for medical facilities, facilities with allergies, or eco-conscious businesses.
  • How do you handle complaints or quality issues? — A good company has a clear, fast process. A bad one gets defensive.
  • Can you accommodate our schedule? — Can they clean after hours, on weekends, or around your specific business hours?
  • What's included in the scope of service? — Get specifics in writing. Vague agreements lead to disappointment.

Types of Commercial Cleaning Services Available in Phoenix

Commercial cleaning in Phoenix covers a wide range of services. Depending on your property, you may need one or several of the following:

  • Janitorial Cleaning — Regular cleaning of restrooms, floors, trash, break rooms, and common areas
  • Office Cleaning — Workstations, conference rooms, lobbies, and kitchen areas
  • Medical Facility Cleaning — OSHA/CDC-compliant disinfection for clinics and healthcare offices
  • Warehouse Cleaning — Industrial floor scrubbing, loading docks, and high-reach cleaning
  • Carpet Cleaning — Hot water extraction for commercial carpets
  • Pressure Washing — Parking lots, building exteriors, sidewalks, and dumpster pads
  • Window Cleaning — Interior and exterior glass for storefronts and office buildings
  • Post-Construction Cleaning — New builds and renovations cleaned to move-in ready

Why Arizona's Climate Matters for Commercial Cleaning

Phoenix and Maricopa County have unique environmental challenges that affect your commercial property's cleaning needs:

  • Dust storms (haboobs) — These can coat your entire property in a thick layer of dust. Window cleaning and parking lot pressure washing need to be more frequent than in other states.
  • Extreme heat — Line striping and sealcoating need to be done in cooler morning hours. Cleaning products also behave differently in 115°F heat.
  • Hard water — Maricopa County has some of the hardest water in the US. Mineral deposits from sprinklers build up on windows and surfaces and need specialized treatment.
  • Monsoon season — Summer monsoons leave behind debris, water damage, and dirty surfaces that need prompt attention.

A cleaning company with experience in the Phoenix market understands these challenges and plans around them. A company from out of state or one that works primarily in other climates may not.

How Much Does Commercial Cleaning Cost in Phoenix?

Commercial cleaning pricing in Phoenix varies based on the type of service, size of your facility, and frequency of cleaning. Here are some general ranges:

  • Office cleaning — Typically $0.08–$0.15 per square foot per visit, depending on scope
  • Janitorial service — Usually quoted as a monthly rate based on frequency and facility size
  • Pressure washing — Parking lots typically range from $150–$600+ depending on size
  • Window cleaning — Commercial properties are typically quoted by number of panes/stories
  • Post-construction cleaning — Usually $0.15–$0.35 per square foot depending on project complexity

Be cautious of prices that seem extremely low. In commercial cleaning, you generally get what you pay for. Underpaid, undertrained staff leads to inconsistent results and high turnover.
