What We Do

Here's Exactly What's Included

A proper strip and wax is a multi-step process. Here's everything we do to bring your floors back to life.

Stripping the Old Finish

  • Old wax and finish removed completely
  • Commercial stripper applied and agitated
  • Floor scrubbed with a rotary machine
  • Residue fully wet-vacuumed and rinsed

Deep Cleaning the Surface

  • Floor scrubbed and neutralized after stripping
  • Grout lines and edges cleaned
  • Scuffs and embedded dirt removed
  • Floor allowed to dry fully before finishing

Applying Fresh Wax Coats

  • Commercial-grade floor finish applied
  • Multiple coats for depth and durability
  • Each coat dried before the next is applied
  • Uniform, streak-free finish throughout

Buffing & Burnishing

  • High-speed burnisher used for maximum shine
  • Removes light scratches and scuffs from top layer
  • Keeps floors looking freshly waxed between recoats
  • Available as a standalone maintenance service

Floor Types We Service

  • VCT (vinyl composition tile) — most common
  • Luxury vinyl plank and tile (LVP/LVT)
  • Linoleum and sheet vinyl
  • Concrete with floor finish applied

Ongoing Floor Maintenance

  • Regular buffing to maintain shine
  • Recoat (add wax without stripping) as needed
  • Full strip and wax annually or as required
  • Custom programs to keep floors protected
Why Choose Velo

Why Commercial Properties in Maricopa County Choose Us

Floor stripping and waxing looks simple but it's easy to get wrong — streaky finish, uneven shine, or floors that look worse after the job. We use the right products, the right equipment, and the right process every time.

01

We Strip All the Way Down.

Some companies cut corners and apply new wax over old buildup. That creates a yellow, uneven finish that gets worse over time. We strip the floor completely first — every time — so the new finish adheres properly and looks great.

02

We Work Overnight.

Stripping and waxing takes time and the floor needs to be clear while we work. We schedule all floor jobs for evenings or weekends so your business is never interrupted. Floors are dry and ready when you open.

03

Waxed Floors Are Easier to Clean.

A proper wax finish creates a protective barrier that dirt sits on top of — instead of getting ground into the floor. That means your regular cleaning is faster and more effective, and the floor stays looking better longer.

04

We Handle the Whole Floor Program.

Strip and wax once, then we handle regular buffing and recoating to maintain the finish. One vendor manages your entire floor care program — no juggling different companies for different services.

Floor Stripping & Waxing FAQ

Common questions about hard floor care for commercial properties in Maricopa County.

Most commercial floors need a full strip and recoat once or twice a year. In between, regular buffing and spot recoating keeps the finish looking fresh. High-traffic floors in retail stores, schools, or hospitals may need full stripping more frequently. We'll assess your floors and recommend the right schedule.

Stripping removes all old wax and finish — it's the most intensive process and the starting point for a proper floor restoration. Waxing applies fresh coats of floor finish to protect the surface and create shine. Buffing uses a high-speed machine to polish the existing wax layer, bringing back the shine without applying new coats. All three are part of a complete floor care program.

We work on VCT (vinyl composition tile), standard vinyl, linoleum, sheet vinyl, and concrete with a floor finish. We do not strip and wax hardwood, natural stone, ceramic tile, or polished concrete — those surfaces require different care methods. If you're not sure what type of floor you have, just describe it to us and we'll advise you.
