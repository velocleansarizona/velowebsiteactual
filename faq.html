Arizona Specific

Arizona's dust storms (haboobs) and dry, windy climate mean windows, parking lots, and building exteriors get dirty faster than in other states. We recommend more frequent window cleaning and pressure washing for Maricopa County properties — typically every 4–6 weeks for windows and quarterly for exterior surfaces. Our team is experienced with Arizona-specific conditions.

Yes. Monsoon season in Arizona can leave a significant mess — debris, flooding, damaged landscaping, and dirty surfaces. We offer post-monsoon cleanup as a one-time service or as part of your regular maintenance plan. Just give us a call after a storm and we'll get your property back in shape quickly.

Yes. Maricopa County has some of the hardest water in the country, and mineral deposits from sprinkler overspray are one of the most common window cleaning challenges we deal with. We use specialized mineral deposit removers to treat calcium and lime buildup and restore your glass before the damage becomes permanent.

Yes. Most commercial properties in Maricopa County have a mix of desert rock, decomposed granite, desert plants, and some turf. We handle all of it — raking and refreshing rock, desert plant and cactus maintenance, shrub and tree trimming, weed control, and irrigation maintenance. We understand Arizona's desert climate and what works here.
